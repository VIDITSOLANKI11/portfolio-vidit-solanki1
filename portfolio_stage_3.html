01 — About

Who I am

I'm a second-year B.Tech student in Computer Science with AI/ML at Acropolis Institute of Technology and Research, Indore. My deepest interests lie not in applications, but in the theory underneath — why algorithms work, what problems are fundamentally hard, and how mathematics structures computation itself.

"The question is not just how to solve a problem, but whether it can be solved at all."

I'm preparing rigorously for GATE (CS/IT) with the goal of pursuing a research degree at the Indian Statistical Institute, Kolkata — one of India's premier institutions for theoretical computer science and mathematics.

Beyond academics, I solve 150+ algorithmic problems on Codeforces, LeetCode, and HackerRank, and independently study graduate-level texts like CLRS and Sipser.
